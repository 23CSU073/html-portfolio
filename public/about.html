About Me

My adaptability and love for learning help me tackle new challenges with confidence, whether it’s debugging code, designing intuitive interfaces, or exploring new technologies like Salesforce to strengthen my professional expertise.

Balancing this venture with my academic pursuits has shown me how to navigate multiple responsibilities while staying true to my passions.

The intersection of creativity and logic excites me, whether it’s designing a seamless user experience for a web application or experimenting with new baking techniques.
